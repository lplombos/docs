##  Coworker Extension
To allow AI workers read and see your current browser session we have developed Coworker.

Coworker is a browser extension that expand AI workers capabilities even allowing them to operate the browser on your behalf. The main purpose of coworker is increasing productivity and facilitating information flow beteween your workspace and AI proccesses.

## AutoWeb
Browser ML Framework Powered by BOSS (Browser Operation Supervision System) and by decentralized AI. AutoWeb provide various web AI Services from a Web Scraping Agent (to extract structured data from webs with natural languaje queries) to Next-gen RPA to automate browser workflows on Cloud Virtual Browsers and on your own browser (Via Coworker Extension).
